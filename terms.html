Description of Service

FlipperHelper is a personal inventory management application designed for individuals who buy and sell items at markets, car boot sales, and similar venues. The App allows you to:

  • Track purchases with photos, prices, and source information
  • Manage listings across multiple selling platforms
  • Record sales and calculate profits
  • Track business expenses such as entry fees and transport costs
  • Export data to Google Sheets (optional)
  • Back up photos to Google Drive (optional)
